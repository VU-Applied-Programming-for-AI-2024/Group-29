Welcome to MYTEAM

MYTEAM is a unique tool that allows you to follow your and your team games from any device.

Select Team

To never miss your team's game: select the team from the sport and tournament that you want to follow.

Add it to your calendar or your games and be always up to date.

Custom Game

Besides following proffesional sport, add your own games with your friends and share the calendar events with them!

My Game

Here you can see all the games you added. If you don't want to follow a certain game, or added the wrong friendly match, simply delete it.

Other Features

If none of your teams are playing, no problem. Check the live games options, where you will see all the currenlty live games.

If our pre-set dark theme is not for you, try out our light-theme mode.
